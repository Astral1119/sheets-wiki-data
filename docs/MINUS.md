Returns the difference of two numbers. Equivalent to the `-` operator.

### Sample Usage

```gse
MINUS(A2,A3)
MINUS(3,4)
```

### Syntax

```gse
MINUS(value1, value2)
```

- `value1` - The minuend, or number to be subtracted from.
- `value2` - The subtrahend, or number to subtract from `value1`.
